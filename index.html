Common Questions

How long does it take to build a website?

For our Starter package, we typically deliver within 3-5 days. Business packages take about 1-2 weeks, and Premium e-commerce sites take 3-4 weeks depending on the complexity and how quickly you can provide content/feedback.

Do you provide hosting and domain names?

The prices listed are for design and development. We can help you purchase a domain and set up hosting on platforms like Hostinger, AWS, or Vercel depending on your needs. We also offer affordable monthly maintenance plans that cover hosting.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! Over 70% of web traffic in India comes from mobile devices. We design with a "mobile-first" approach, ensuring your site looks and functions perfectly on smartphones, tablets, and desktops.

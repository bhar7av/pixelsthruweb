Last updated: April 24, 2026  |  PixelsThruWeb, India (PAN India)

Short version: We only collect what's needed to build your website and respond to you. We never sell your data. We never spam. Simple as that.

1. Who We Are

PixelsThruWeb is a freelance web development service based in India, operated by Bhargav (hereinafter "we", "us", or "our"). We build websites for businesses and individuals across PAN India. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle the information you share with us when you contact us, fill out a quote form, or use any service we provide.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to serve you:

  • Contact Information: Your name, email address, and phone/WhatsApp number when you fill out our contact form or reach out directly.
  • Project Details: Information you voluntarily provide about your business, project requirements, and goals so we can build the right website for you.
  • Project Code: A unique code assigned to your project used for progress tracking.
  • Payment Reference: UPI transaction IDs or payment confirmations for deposit processing. We do not store credit/debit card numbers.
  • Usage Data (Anonymous): Basic analytics such as page views to understand how people interact with our site. No personal identifiers are tracked.

3. Why We Collect This

We use your information strictly for:

  • Responding to your enquiry or quote request
  • Delivering the agreed web development service
  • Sharing project updates and milestones with you
  • Processing and confirming deposits
  • Improving our website and services

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Period.

We may share limited data only with:

  • Formspree (form processing): Your submitted contact form data passes through Formspree's API. They are GDPR compliant. View their policy →
  • WhatsApp: When you contact us via WhatsApp, your conversation is governed by Meta's Privacy Policy.
  • Payment Processors: UPI transactions are processed through your bank/UPI app, not stored by us.

5. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies. Cookies may be used by:

  • Google Fonts (to load typefaces) — no personal data collected
  • Font Awesome (for icons) — no personal data collected

You can disable cookies in your browser settings without affecting the core experience of our site.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information. Your form submissions are sent via HTTPS-encrypted connections. We do not store sensitive data on our servers. Access to your project and contact information is limited to us (Bhargav) only.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

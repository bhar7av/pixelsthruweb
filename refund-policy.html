Refund Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

Our promise: We believe in fair business. If we can't deliver what was agreed, you get your money back. Period.

1. Initial Deposit (100% Refundable)

We collect a small initial deposit before the discovery call to ensure seriousness from both parties. This deposit is fully refundable if:

  • We decide not to proceed after the discussion
  • You change your mind before work begins
  • We are unable to accommodate your requirements

Refund is processed within 24 hours to the same UPI account.

2. Refund Schedule

3. When You ARE Entitled to a Full Refund

  • We fail to deliver the agreed-upon website within the timeline (with a 7-day grace period)
  • The delivered website significantly deviates from the approved design mockup
  • We are unable to complete the project due to reasons on our end

4. When Refunds Do NOT Apply

  • Client delays providing content/feedback for more than 14 days
  • Client requests a complete redesign after approving the mockup
  • Issues caused by third-party services (hosting, domain, payment gateways)
  • The final project has been reviewed and approved by the client

Email bhargavkoushal17@gmail.com or message on WhatsApp with your project code and reason. We respond within 24 hours.

Our Story

A Tradition of Taste

Spice Route was born from a simple belief — that great food should be accessible to everyone. Starting as a small family kitchen in Mumbai, we've grown into a beloved dining destination known for authentic flavors, warm hospitality, and a commitment to using only the freshest ingredients.

Every dish on our menu is a tribute to India's diverse culinary heritage — from the smoky tandoors of Punjab to the coastal kitchens of Kerala.
